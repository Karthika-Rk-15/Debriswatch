The Problem

Earth's orbit is getting crowded

⟳

What is Space Debris?

Defunct satellites, rocket fragments, and collision-generated particles orbiting Earth at velocities exceeding 7 km/s — each a kinetic threat to active spacecraft.

⚠

Why It Matters

Even a 1cm fragment carries the kinetic energy of a grenade. A single collision can disable a billion-dollar mission and trigger the Kessler Syndrome cascade.

🛡

Our Solution

DebrisWatch AI continuously monitors orbital objects, predicts collision risks, and provides intelligent avoidance recommendations — autonomously, 24/7.
